Join Our Group

Why Join Us

What We Value

We are committed to building an inclusive, rigorous, and supportive research environment where everyone can do their best work, and help solve societal problems.

Computational Excellence

We train students to become expert computational scientists — proficient in broad range of techniques including DFT, MD, ML, and KMC — skills highly valued in both academia and industry.

Intellectual Curiosity

We ask deep questions about how matter behaves at its most fundamental scales, pursuing answers with rigour and creativity even when they aren't clear.

Impact-Driven Science

Our research is funded by SERB, DST, and industry partners because it addresses real-world challenges in energy storage, nano materials, and human health.

Collaboration & Openness

Our work spans physics, chemistry, and biology. We thrive on collaboration within the group, across IIT Bhubaneswar, and with other institutes in India.

Routes

How to Join

Please read the ongoing research projects and publications before writing. A focused email that connects your interests and expertise to the group is the most useful first step.

Fellowship-supported route
Postdoc
Postdoctoral candidates can apply through Institute Postdoctoral Fellowships, NPDF, ICMR/DBT fellowships, and related external fellowship schemes. Write to me with your research interest and background; a proposal can be developed together when there is a strong fit.
Notes
Project-funded postdoctoral positions will be advertised here and on the institute page.
Prior experience in simulations, theory, or scientific computing is useful.
Institute admission route
PhD
PhD applications must go through the IIT Bhubaneswar institute portal. Positions usually open each semester: March-April for the Autumn semester and October-November for the Spring semester.
Who should write
Students with GATE, JRF, or INSPIRE fellowships are welcome to write directly.
Briefly state your interests, background, and the research direction you want to pursue.
IIT Bhubaneswar students
Thesis Project
Thesis and project work for M.Sc. / BTP / ITEP students is open to students of IIT Bhubaneswar. Please go through the ongoing research projects and publications, then schedule a meeting by email and meet in person after deciding the topic of interest.
Before meeting
Identify one or two research directions that match your interests.
Briefly mention relevant coursework, coding experience, or prior project work.
Short-term research route
Internship
Students interested in internships or project work should first go through the ongoing research projects and recent publications. If the work is of interest and you have relevant expertise, write to me directly by email. We are not able to offer any financial support for such positions.
Useful preparation
Mention the paper or project that motivated your inquiry.
Include relevant coursework, coding experience, and prior project work.
Contact for opportunities

Write with a focused research fit.

Please mention the route you are interested in, the research direction or paper that motivated your inquiry, and relevant coursework, coding, or simulation experience.
