Principal Investigator
Dr. Hemant Kumar

Hemant Kumar is an Assistant Professor of Physics at IIT Bhubaneswar, where he leads the SCALE group. His research spans computational materials physics, nanomaterials, biophysics of the genome, and materials for energy storage — unified by multiscale simulation methods from DFT to mesoscale.
