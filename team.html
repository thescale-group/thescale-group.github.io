Principal Investigator

Dr. Hemant Kumar

Principal Investigator · Associate Professor

Dr. Hemant Kumar leads the SCALE group at IIT Bhubaneswar, focusing on multiscale computational methods for energy materials, genome biophysics, and 2D quantum materials. He received his PhD from IISc Bangalore and completed postdoctoral training at the University of Pennsylvania with Prof. Vivek Shenoy. He joined IIT Bhubaneswar in 2019.
