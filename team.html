Group Members

Dr. Hemant Kumar

Principal Investigator · Assistant Professor

Dr. Kumar received his Ph.D. from IISc Bangalore and completed postdoctoral research at the University of Pennsylvania. He leads the SCALE Group, bridging computational materials and biophysics.

Primary Methodology

Employing density functional theory (DFT), molecular dynamics (MD), and kinetic Monte Carlo methods to investigate material properties at atomic scales.

Ph.D. Candidate (Since 2019)

Pratik has a strong background in optics and photonics. His goal is to bridge the gap between fundamental optical physics and real-world medical applications.

Focus: Photonic Sensors

SB

Saheb Bera

Ph.D. Candidate (Since 2020)

Saheb specializes in computational condensed matter physics. He utilizes first-principles DFT calculations to predict the stability and functional properties of novel 2D materials.

Focus: MXenes & Spintronics

Understanding the phase stability of MXenes and the role of coordination symmetries and surface terminations.

SS

Swayamshree Senapati

Ph.D. Candidate (Since 2020)

Swayamshree brings physics-based approaches to complex biological problems. She is interested in how physical mechanics within the cell nucleus regulate genetic output.

Focus: Biophysics & Genome Architecture

Studying the fundamental correlation between 3D chromatin structure and gene expression.

Sidhant Barik

Focus: Battery Electrolytes
