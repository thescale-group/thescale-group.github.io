SCALE Group

SCALE stands for Simulation of Complex Systems Across Length and Energy. We use electronic-structure theory, molecular dynamics, statistical mechanics, and machine-learning models to connect microscopic interactions with function in energy materials, interfaces, and living systems.

Research

Research Highlights

Ion Transport

Solvation and ionic transport

Liquid and solid electrolytes are studied through solvation structure, ion diffusion, and interfacial transport mechanisms.

Material Design

Battery materials and mechanisms

Mechanistic insight is used to design electrodes and electrolytes for next-generation batteries, with emphasis on sodium-metal and sodium-sulfur systems.

Data-Driven Design

Machine learning for materials

Data, neural-network potentials, and simulation-derived descriptors accelerate discovery and optimization of battery materials.

Gene Regulation

Genome organization and regulation

Polymer models, kinetic modelling, and chromosome-structure data connect genome organization with transcriptional regulation.
